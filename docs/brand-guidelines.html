06 — Voice & Tone
Writing Style
✓ Do

Warm, direct, human. Like a clever friend who knows tech.

Short sentences. Active voice. Concrete specifics.

Celebrate the personal — this is your handwriting.

"Speak naturally. Watch your words appear in your handwriting."
✗ Don't

Cold or corporate. No "leverage", "synergy", "seamless".

Over-explaining. Trust the user to get it.

Exclamation marks everywhere. One at most per page.

"Leverage our cutting-edge AI-powered voice recognition to seamlessly generate personalized handwriting-style text outputs."
